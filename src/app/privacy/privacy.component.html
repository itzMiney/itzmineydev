If you contact us via the email address provided on the website, we may store your message and contact information in order to respond to your inquiry or provide the requested service. This data is treated as confidential and will not be shared with third parties without your consent.

c) Third-Party Services (Cloudflare)

We use Cloudflare, Inc. to protect and accelerate our website. Cloudflare provides services such as DDoS mitigation, secure DNS resolution, and performance optimization. As part of providing these services, Cloudflare may process technical data such as your IP address and HTTP request headers. This processing is done solely for security and operational purposes and occurs automatically when you access the website.

Cloudflare acts as a data processor on our behalf. For more information, please see Cloudflare’s Privacy Policy.
